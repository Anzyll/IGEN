About

iGEN is an intercollegiate IT - cultural fest organised and hosted by the department of computer science Sri C Achutha menon Govt college Thrissur. This year the fest is to be happened on February 19 2025 with almost 9 IT and non IT event and a closing event at the end of the fest . Our fest not only focus on IT participants but also the participants for the non IT background. This is Homepage, You can feel participate this program.
